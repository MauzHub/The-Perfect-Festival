About The Perfect Festival

The Perfect Festival is a free booking game. Spin a real festival and year — Glastonbury 2019, Coachella 2018, Tomorrowland — draft one act at a time from that edition, and build an 11-stage line-up: three headliners and a full undercard. The game projects your bill into a festival score and a touring run. Book a line-up strong enough and every date sells out: a flawless, sold-out tour.

How it works

  1. Spin to draw a real festival and year.
  2. Draft one act from that edition onto an open stage.
  3. Place your biggest acts as headliners — headline stages count for the most.
  4. Fill all 11 stages until the poster is complete.
  5. Get your festival score & tour — and the tier your bill earns, from an Open-Mic Night up to the legendary Perfect Festival.

Stages

Any act can play any stage — stages differ by size, not genre. Three headliners carry the most weight, the main stages less, the undercard least. So the real decision is who you put up top: a weak headliner drags the whole bill, while loading your biggest names into the headline slots is what carries a line-up. The same artist can't appear twice, even across different festivals. A line-up with genuine genre range also scores a little higher than a one-note bill.

The sold-out tour

The ultimate result is a bill so strong that it sells out every date of the tour. It is achievable but deliberately rare — even a perfectly drafted line-up only manages it now and then. Most bills land somewhere between a mid-bill weekend and a main-stage triumph.

Frequently asked questions

Is The Perfect Festival free to play?
Yes — it is completely free and runs in your browser. There is nothing to download.
Which festivals can I draw?
A spread of well-known festivals and editions across rock, pop, electronic, hip-hop and R&B, each with a roster of acts and a draw score. It is an unofficial fan game — line-ups are illustrative, not official.
Can you really sell out the whole tour?
Yes, but it is rare. You need top acts headlining and real range across the bill. Most line-ups land between a mid-bill weekend and a main-stage finish.
How is my festival score worked out?
It is a weighted average of your acts' draw power — headliners count most — plus a small bonus for genre range, with a little night-to-night variation on the tour.
Do I need an account?
No sign-up and no app needed — just spin and book.
